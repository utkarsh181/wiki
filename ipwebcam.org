#+TITLE: To use android phone as webcam

* To connect to device
  adb devices
  For more: https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Android_Debug_Bridge
* To forward to localhost
adb forward tcp:8081 tcp:8080
* IpWebcam Settings
http://localhost:8081/
* Some important ip addresses 
http://localhost:8081/audio.wav
http://localhost:8081/video
